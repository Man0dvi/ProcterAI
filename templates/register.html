Student Registration

Instructions:

  • 1) Fill the details(your name, id and password) first.
  • 2) Click on 'Start Scan' button to start scanning your face.
  • 3) Keep your face in front of the camera and sit in a well lit room.
  • 4) Press 's' to capture an image when your face is properly aligned.
  • 5) For every next picture, move your face slightly to the left and right for different angles.
  • 6) Make sure to capture 20 images in total for accurate recognition.
  • 7) Once done, click on 'Finish' button
  • 8) After getting the message "You are registered", Click on 'Save Student' button
